connecting-line2: width & dash units — orthographic, auto-zooming

The camera zoom cycles automatically. Watch how each line responds:

  • top (cyan) — width in px, dashUnits:auto (resolves to px under an ortho camera): width and dashes stay screen-constant as you zoom.
  • middle (yellow) — width in m, dashUnits:auto (resolves to m): width and dashes scale with the world — they grow as you zoom in.
  • bottom (red) — width in px but dashUnits forced to m: screen-constant width, world-scaling dashes.

High-DPR hairline: the white width:1 px line stays a clean ~1px line; the width:0.5 px one renders fainter, not absent.

Perspective behaviour is shown on units-perspective.html.
