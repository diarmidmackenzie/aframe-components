connecting-line2: dash units — perspective

Use WASD to move toward / away and the mouse to look around. Both lines face you at the same distance. As you walk up to them:

  • top (cyan) — dashUnits:auto (resolves to world units under a perspective camera): a fixed number of dashes anchored in the world. They grow on screen along with the line — stable and natural. This is the comfortable VR default.
  • bottom (red) — dashUnits forced to px: each dash stays a constant size on screen, so as the line fills more of your view more dashes appear — the disorienting effect that auto avoids under perspective.

Orthographic / zoom behaviour is shown on units-ortho.html.
