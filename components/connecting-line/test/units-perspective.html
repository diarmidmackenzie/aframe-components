connecting-line2: dash units — perspective approach

The camera moves toward and away from two lines that are angled in depth (near end at the front, far end behind). Watch the dashes as it approaches:

  • top (cyan) — dashUnits:auto (resolves to world units under a perspective camera): dashes are world-anchored — stable, and they foreshorten naturally with the line's depth. This is the comfortable default for VR.
  • bottom (red) — dashUnits forced to px: the dashes shrink/grow as the camera approaches — the disorienting effect that auto deliberately avoids under perspective.

Note on the forced-px line: world-per-pixel is approximated at the line's midpoint depth, so the dash density is uniform along each line at any instant (it does not drift near-vs-far within one line). What changes is the overall density as the camera's distance to the midpoint changes frame-to-frame.

Orthographic / zoom behaviour is shown on units-ortho.html.
