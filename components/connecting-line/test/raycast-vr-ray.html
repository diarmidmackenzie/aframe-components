connecting-line2: VR ray — m-distance raycast (perspective camera)

Move the mouse over each line; WASD to walk toward / away. Both are width:1 units:px 1px strokes (a controller-ray primitive). The cursor carries raycaster-thresholds="line:0.05", so params.Line2.threshold = 0.05m (via the default matchLine mode).

  • cyan — raycastUnits:m: a fixed world pick band (~5cm). Grabbable at near and far depth; the tolerance does not shrink as you back away. This is the headline VR-ray proof.
  • red — raycastUnits:px (stock screen-space): the 1px stroke's pick band is screen-space; at distance it stays ~1px on screen but the world tolerance shrinks with depth. Compare grab feel to cyan.

Hover tints the line yellow. Walk up close to the far ends and back away to feel the difference between a world-constant (cyan) and screen-space (red) pick band on an identical thin stroke.
