connecting-line2: VR ray — m-distance raycast (perspective camera)

Move the mouse over the line; WASD to walk toward / away. It is a width:1 units:px 1px stroke (a controller-ray primitive). The cursor carries raycaster-thresholds="line:0.05", so params.Line2.threshold = 0.05m (via the default matchLine mode).

  • cyan — raycastUnits:m: a fixed world pick band (~5cm). Grabbable at near and far depth; the tolerance does not shrink as you back away. This is the headline VR-ray proof.

px is not shown here. A single raycaster carries one params.Line2.threshold (0.05 from line:0.05), which a raycastUnits:px line would read as 0.05 pixels — unpickable at every depth, and it can't share this m-cursor anyway. The px path needs its own px-threshold cursor; see raycast-screen.html.

Hover tints the line yellow and updates the status line below. Walk up close to the far end and back away — the world-constant (cyan) band stays grabbable the whole way.
